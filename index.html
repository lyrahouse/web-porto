About Me

I'm Layla — I started as a 3D designer, building spaces and concepts you can almost step into. Later, I found myself drawn to graphic design and UI/UX, turning visuals into experiences people can interact with. When I'm not designing, I'm usually writing. Stories, characters, messy drafts — and once, one of my short stories even made it into a published book. To me, designing and writing feel the same: both are about making people feel something.

This is where I share both sides of my work. Welcome.

Services

What I Offer

3D Model SketchUp

I offer 3D modeling services using SketchUp to bring your concepts to life — from event setups and exhibition spaces to interior layouts and architectural structures. Each model is built with precision and attention to scale, helping you visualize the space clearly before execution.

Graphic Design

I provide graphic design services for a variety of visual needs — including posters, packaging, book layouts, logos, and more. My approach combines strong composition, thoughtful typography, and cohesive branding to create designs that are both functional and visually compelling.

Creative Writing

I offer creative writing services including novel writing, ghostwriting, and scriptwriting for film, drama, or short content. Whether you're looking to develop a compelling narrative, need help bringing your characters to life, or want to collaborate on story ideas, I’m available for both writing and consultation. Let's turn your concepts into impactful stories with structure, depth, and emotional resonance.

Icon Design

I specialize in icon design for both digital and physical use, including systems like wayfinding and signage. With a focus on clarity, consistency, and visual language, I create icons that enhance usability and strengthen visual identity — whether for apps, branding materials, or environmental graphics.

Photography

I offer photography services focused on capturing moments with intention — from product shots and portraits to event documentation. With an eye for composition, lighting, and visual storytelling, I aim to create images that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also communicate mood, message, and meaning.

UI/UX Design

I design user interfaces and experiences that are intuitive, visually cohesive, and user-centered. From wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes, I focus on creating layouts that are easy to navigate and aligned with user behavior — ensuring your website or app is not only functional but also engaging and accessible.
